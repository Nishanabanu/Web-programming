What is the MCA Full Form?

The MCA full form is Master of Computer Application, is a postgraduate degree programme which focuses on computer applications and computer science and programming languages.

What is MCA?

Master of Computer Application is a two-year postgraduate level course which delves deeper into the specialisations in the branch of computer applications.
